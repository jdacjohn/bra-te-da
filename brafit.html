Bra fitting is an exact / inexact science. Really, only 3 measurements are required but a true bra fitter has just begun at that point. Now, we don't want you to think this is an all day process. Bra fitting actually is fun. Especially when you see the results. Lots of ladies get compliments saying they look like they've lost weight. Some are asked if they've had some "Work" done. Still most just walk away with a feeling of a more positive self image because their bra actually flatters their figure.
 
Why the proper fit matters to your breast health
The brazier is not meant to be work 24 hours a day. In fact it can be unhealthy because your breasts are not allowed to have natural freedom. What's more, still more women wear bras that fit poorly, leave indentions in their skin and breast tissue and generally cause discomfort. The rule is, if it hurts don't wear it.
 
Shopping for the right bra takes a little patience and a small amount of quality time at Bra Te Da. The bra band must fit snugly around the chest, just below the breast, so as to distribute the weight around your body. The shoulder straps should not bear the weight because that starts a series of symptoms where the straps dig into your shoulders and cause a loose fitting band to ride up. The way a bra should work is the cups should hold and shape your breasts so your shoulder straps do not dig in causing neck pain, and all day discomfort.
